1.Which are the products supplied by Leka Trading and the category Name.

SELECT p.ProductName,c.CategoryName
FROM Products p
JOIN Suppliers s
ON s.SupplierID = p.SupplierID
AND SupplierName LIKE 'Leka%'
JOIN Categories c
ON p.CategoryID = c.CategoryID;

2.Which is the highest ordered product.

SELECT p.ProductName,SUM(o.Quantity) as MaxOrder
FROM Products p
JOIN OrderDetails o 
ON p.ProductID=o.ProductID
GROUP BY ProductName
ORDER BY MaxOrder DESC
LIMIT 1;
